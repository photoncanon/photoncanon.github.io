Short Bio

  • I joined SenseTime Group Limited after graduation from CUHK as a senior researcher. My goal is to solve real world problems with computer vision techniques. Currently I mainly focus on autonomous driving, medical imaging tasks.

  • I obtained a Ph.D. degree in the department of Electronic Engineering of The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK), supervised by Professor Xiaogang Wang . I was both in IVP lab and Multimedia Lab.

  • Before I came to CUHK, I received a Bachelor's degree from the department of Optical Engineering of Zhejiang University (ZJU) in July 2012.
